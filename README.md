## About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Dalhousie University, working at the intersection of:
- `LLM Reasoning` (stability, drift, order sensitivity)
- `Explainable AI` and interpretability diagnostics
- `Bias & Fairness` in LLMs and recommender systems
- `Visualization` for model behavior and decision processes

I enjoy turning research ideas into practical systems and open-source tools.

## Research + Build Highlights

- **Context order and reasoning drift**: measuring sensitivity from token probabilities
- **AIS analytics**: building data-driven maritime analysis and visualization workflows
- **Multi-agent simulation**: sandboxing social behaviors in interactive environments
- **Open tooling**: shipping practical utilities and developer-facing products
